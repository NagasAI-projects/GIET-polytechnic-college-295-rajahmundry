**Welcome To GIET Polytechnic College**
Empowering students for a better future...
GIET Polytechnic College is dedicated to providing quality technical education and shaping students into skilled professionals. With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-driven training, we prepare students for successful careers in engineering and technology. GIET polytechnic college was established in 2009 to offer engineering education of world-class standards. Over the years, the college has come a long way to gain a place of repute and a preferred destination for quality engineering education. Located in the serene and sylvan suburbs of Chaitanya Nagar, Rajahmundry on NH-16, the sprawling 300-acre campus of GIET campus reflects the finest educational facilities around. The stately 5-storeyed structure set amid idyllic setting ensures a most congenial atmosphere for scholastic pursuit in right earnest. An ISO-certified,NBA-accredited and NAAC-A Grade accredited institution, GIET has many distinctions to its credit.
